## How to Add Product Images

1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/
2. Login with admin/admin123
3. Navigate to Products > Products
4. Click on a product
5. Scroll to "Product images" section
6. Click "Add another Product Image"
7. Upload an image and check "Is primary"
8. Save

The site will automatically display uploaded images instead of icon placeholders!
